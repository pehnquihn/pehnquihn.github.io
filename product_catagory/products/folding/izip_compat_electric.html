Description

Electric meets folding bike with the 2015 Izip E3 Compact. Super portable and easy to use, this folding bike fits short to tall people and is easily adjusted to go from one to the other or anywhere in between. It quickly folds up for easy transport and storage making it great for commuting, throwing into a car trunk or taking it most anywhere. A rear rack is included to carry your purchases and small items. Unfold the E3 Compact and discover urban freedom.
