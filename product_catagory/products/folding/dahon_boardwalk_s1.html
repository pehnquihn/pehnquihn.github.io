Dahon 2014 Boardwalk S1 Folding Bike
$299.99
Description

The 2014 Dahon Boardwalk S1 Folding Bike is the perfect example of simple being better. The single-speed drivetrain is light and low-maintenance. The Boardwalk S1 is a remarkable folding ride that features a rear coaster brake and front V-Brake with durable aluminum 20 inch Wheels. These components matched to a strong folding steel frame create a simple folding bike that is an excellent choice for urban commuters who need to be able to stash their bike. A rear rack, full fenders and kickstand round out the Boardwalk S1.
