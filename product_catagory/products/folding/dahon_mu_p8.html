Dahon 2014 Mu P8 Folding Bike
$749.99
Description

The 2014 Dahon Mu P8 Folding Bike, built on one of their most popular platforms, is a star performer when it comes to value, performance and weight. Appropriate for all kinds of rides, this Dahon is geared to be fast, stylish and perfect for city errands or country jaunts. Quick and easy folding means this is a good option for urban commuters who need to jump on a bus or train for part of their ride, and the lightweight aluminum frame means it is easy to carry this folding bike when needed.
