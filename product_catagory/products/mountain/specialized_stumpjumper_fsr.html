Specialized 2014 Stumpjumper FSR Comp 29er Full Suspension Mountain Bike
$2,599.99
Description

The 2014 Specialized Stumpjumper FSR Comp M5 Mountain Bike is a balanced bike that performs well on everything from steep climbs to flowy singletrack to technical descents. With 130mm of plush and efficient FSR suspension, and a custom Fox Float CTD Evolution shock with AUTOSAG, an air-sprung RockShox Revelation RC3 140mm fork, and custom SRAM 2x10 XC Trail drivetrain, the 2014 Stumpjumper FSR Comp Full Suspension MTB wants to show you some good times on any trail you can find.
