Specialized 2014 Camber Base 29er Full Suspension Mountain Bike
$1,699.99
Description

Designed to get out and explore, the 2014 Specialized Camber is a classic well rounded trail bike every rider will enjoy. With a custom X-Fusion O2 RL shock, Tektro Draco 2 hydraulic disc brakes, and Shimano 9-speed drivetrain, the Camber 29 create a lightweight full suspension bike that is comfortable on any trail.
