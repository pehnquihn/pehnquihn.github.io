Raleigh 2015 Tokul 3 Mountain Bike
$1,049.99
Description

The 2015 Raleigh Tokul 3 Mountain Bike is designed to attack both the ups and downs found on today’s newer bike specific trails. With a roomy cockpit pedaling is made easy, while the longer travel fork and slackened geometry propel you down the trail. Versatile and playful these bikes are created to be more than your average hardtail. With 27.5 inch wheels the Tokul is s capable of rolling over rocks and roots with ease just like the larger 29er wheel, yet nimble in hard to navigate situations. These elements combine to make a wheel adept in any kind of terrain and in all conditions. The 6061 Aluminum used to construct the frame provides a durable and compliant platform for trail use. A Shimano Deore drivetrain paired with Shimano hydraulic disc brakes provide the functionality and dependability needed for off road use. Spec’ed with a Rockshox 30 Gold 120mm fork rough terrain is ready to be conquered.
