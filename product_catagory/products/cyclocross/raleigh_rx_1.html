Raleigh 2012 RX 1.0 Cyclocross Bike
$1,199.99
Description

Be ready for the ‘cross season with the 2012 Raleigh RX 1.0 Cyclocross bike. An excellent introduction to the sport, the RX 1.0 features an Atomic 13 SL Butted Aluminum frame, Easton X EC70 Carbon Fiber Fork, Tektro Canti brakes and Weinmann DP18 Double Wall rims. Add to that the SRAM Apex 20 speed Drivetrain and FSA Gossamer BB30 Crankset and you have a ‘cross bike that is ready to tear up the dirt.
