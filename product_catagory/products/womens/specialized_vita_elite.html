Specialized 2014 Vita Elite Road Bike
$749.99
Description

The 2014 Specialized Vita Elite Women's Flat Bar Road Bike offers the speed of a road bike with the comfort and confidence that comes from a flat bar, for exercising, commuting, or hitting the open road. A lightweight carbon fork and 27-speed Shimano gearing means lighter, smoother, and greater efficiency over varied terrain. The light, durable and efficient E5 Aluminum Alloy frame is optimized for speed and comfort. The Sirrus double-wall rims are strong enough to handle urban obstacles and are light for quick acceleration.
