Specialized 2015 Hotrock 20 inch Coaster Brake Boys Kids Bike
$249.99
Description

The perfect first ‘real’ bike, the 2015 Specialized Hotrock 20 Coaster Brake Boy’s Bike will have your little tike cruising the neighborhood in no time. Sized to be easy to ride and comfortable, the lightweight frame makes it so much easier for kids to ride. By speccing this bike with both a coaster brake and a rear hand brake, Specialized have made sure that your kid can safely learn how to use hand brakes in their own time. Finish the bike off in bright, bold and fun colors and you have a bike that will get your kid away from the TV and out riding every chance they get.
