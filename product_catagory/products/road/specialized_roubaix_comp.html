Specialized 2014 Roubaix Comp Road Race Bike
$2,599.99
Description

The smoother, faster 2014 Specialized Roubaix Comp Road Bike - named after the grueling Paris-Roubaix race it has won three consecutive times - is a high-performance, endurance-focused bike that's highly stable and efficient. The light, stiff and compliant FACT 8r Carbon Fiber Frame is a road enthusiasts dream come true. Jump on the Roubaix and you will notice how quickly it accelerates, how efficient it feels, but more than any other racing bike, how stable and comfortable it feels at the same time. Specialized have built up the 2014 Roubaix Comp with New Shimano 105/Ultegra 11-speed compact gearing and Fulcrum S5 wheels lower weight and higher performance.
