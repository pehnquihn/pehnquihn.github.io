Specialized 2014 Allez Base Road Race Bike
$769.99
Description

If you’re thinking about getting into cycling and need your first road bike, the stiff, light, race inspired 2014 Specialized Allez Road bike is a great place to start. Featuring a Shimano Claris drive train and light weight Axis wheels to help improve efficiency on any terrain. The 2014 Allez makes for a perfect Winter training bike, or an awesome starter bike that won’t leave your wallet feeling light.
