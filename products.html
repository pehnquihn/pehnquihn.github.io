What Bike is Right for You?

Each type of bike has its own advantages and disadvantages. If you are new to the hobby, take time to think of what you would like to do with your bike. Decide if you will be using your bike for racing or leisure. You should also consider what type of path you will be riding on; different bikes are built for different terrains. Lastly, set your price range to the budget that fits you best.


Road Bikes

Road bikes have a focus on efficiency and speed. Most Road bikes are very comfortable and a pleasure to ride. If racing is for you, road bikes are the way to go.

Go to Road Bikes
Mountain Bikes

Mountain Bikes are the kings of off-road riding. They are built to take any type of path you ride it on. If you are looking to take your bike to extreme places this is the perfect place to start.

Go to Mountain Bikes
Cyclocross Bikes

Cyclocross is a very demanding sport; therefore, it demands a top notch bike. These bikes are very good on and off roads and any incline or decline you will ride on. Cyclocross bikes are the perfect mix of rugged build along with a lightweight package. If you want to participate in this high endurance sport these bikes are a must have.

Go to Cyclocross Bikes
Folding Bikes

Folding bikes are a great alternative to the traditional bike for those who commute by bike and public transport. They fold up into a lightweight small package that can be carried with you as you travel. Due to the size of the folding bike when it is folded and their high quality design, they are also very nice for those on the go.

Go to Folding Bikes
Electric-Assist Bikes

Electric-Assist bikes are very versatile. They are becoming very popular with people in urban areas. Many people are starting to use them to replace their cars when they are commuting to work or running errands. Electric-assist bikes allow people to travel at faster speeds with little work. Women's Bikes

Woman's bikes are tailored to the smaller bodies of women. They are also lighterweight and come in fashionable color schemes. These bikes come in many shapes and forms for nearly every need.

Go to Woman's Bikes
Children's Bikes

These bikes are a great way to get your kids active and outdoors. They come in many different sizes and forms whether your child is a first time or seasoned rider.
