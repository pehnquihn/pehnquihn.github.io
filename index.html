About Us

Cycle Fitness is just starting out in New York City. We would enjoy sharing our love for the wonderful sport of cycling with you. Our staff are all very knowledgeable with many years of experience in the world of cycling. During your visit we will set you up with everything you need to get out and hit the trails right away. We also offer seminars and guided trips to get you to feel as comfortable as possible in your new adventure of of cycling. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us.

Our Mission

Here at Cycle Fitness we strive to share the passion for cycling that we have. Our goal is to help all of our customers start out on the right path to a healthier life style through cycling.
