Central Park Tour

This famous park located right in the middle of the city offers an interesting ride for both beginners and seasoned riders. The six mile long loop located around the park allows riders to start from many entry locations as well as have great places to stop for the view and for picking up something to eat. Whether you have just started biking or are looking for a challenge, this tour will get you started on the right pedal!

Westside Greenway Path Tour

Starting at the Staten Island Ferry and finishing at the George Washington Bridge, this tour will expose you to many new exciting views of the city, Hudson River, and beautiful riverside parks. This path is perfect for the aspiring biker, as traffic is usually low and mainly encompasses other pedestrians. Expect an hour or more of bike riding as this tour packs as much in as possible!

Prospect Park Tour

Although this park is smaller than its brother in Manhattan, this trip packs in just as much experiences. At approximately 3 miles, the loop is about half the length of Central Park. Special attention is held to the surrounding area as well, where the landscape offers rolling hills, flats, and serene settings. Whether or not you have completed the Central Park Tour, this tour is perfect for all!

Carroll Gardens to Coney Island

Home to one of the first bike paths in the United States, this tour caters to both the new and experienced rider. Although in the past some of the path has been demolished for expressways, this path continues to offer its glory of scenery. Whether you are looking for a safer tour or are a history lover, this tour is perfect for all!
